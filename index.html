About Lussohomey

A Family Craft, Three Generations Deep

Founded in 1974, Lussohomey began as a single workbench and a belief that furniture should outlive its maker. Today the atelier is led by Ali Jameel, whose father and grandfather trained him in the same joinery, the same oils, the same patience.

Behind him is a team of designers, joiners, upholsterers, and finishers — many of whom have been with the workshop for decades. Every commission is still drawn, joined, and finished within our own walls, one piece at a time.

Ali Jameel
