For Builders & Contractors

A Manufacturing Partner Behind Your Projects

When you hand over a building, the furniture inside it carries your name as much as ours. We work with builders and contractors as a production partner — trade pricing, one point of contact, and delivery timed to your programme, not ours.

The Trade Programme

Why Contractors Work With Us

Trade Pricing
Project volumes are quoted on trade terms, not retail. The larger and longer the engagement, the better the rates — structured so your margin survives the fit‑out.
One Point of Contact
Every project is assigned a dedicated manager who sits between your site team and our workshop — one call for drawings, approvals, revisions, and delivery.
Built to Your Programme
Production is scheduled in batches against your construction timeline — floor by floor, phase by phase — so furniture arrives when the site is ready, not before or after.
After‑Sales, On Site
Handover isn't the end. Site visits, adjustments, touch‑ups, and repairs are part of the engagement — backed by our lifetime structural guarantee, so your client calls us, not you.
Long‑Term Partnership

A Relationship That Compounds

We would rather furnish your next ten projects than win one. Returning partners get priority production slots, preferential terms that improve with each engagement, and a workshop that already knows your standards, your paperwork, and your sites.

Many of the builders we work with today started with a single show home. The ones who came back did so because handovers were clean, snag lists were short, and their clients were the ones doing the recommending.

How It Works

From BOQ to Handover

01
Consultation & BOQ
We review your drawings, room schedules, and budget, and prepare a furniture bill of quantities against the project.
02
Trade Quotation
A line‑itemed quotation on trade terms, with options at more than one price point per room where useful.
03
Sampling & Approvals
Finish boards, fabric swatches, and a sample piece where the project warrants it — signed off before production begins.
04
Phased Production
Batches built and delivered to your construction schedule, with your project manager reporting progress throughout.
05
Installation & After‑Sales
White‑glove placement, a joint snag walk, and ongoing after‑sales service under our lifetime guarantee.
